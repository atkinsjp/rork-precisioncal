Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Atkins Media ("we," "us") uses cookies and similar technologies on the PrecisionCal website. The PrecisionCal mobile app does not use browser cookies, but it may use comparable local-storage mechanisms described below.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site function correctly, remember preferences, and understand how it is used.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

  • Strictly necessary: required for core functionality such as page navigation and secure access. These cannot be disabled.
  • Preferences: remember non-essential choices like theme or language.
  • Analytics: help us understand which pages are useful so we can improve the experience. These are aggregated and do not identify you personally.
  • Embedded forms: our support page may include a form embedded from GoHighLevel (GHL), which may set its own cookies subject to GHL's privacy practices.

3. Managing Cookies

Most browsers let you refuse or delete cookies via their settings. Disabling strictly necessary cookies may prevent parts of the site from working as intended.

4. Mobile App Storage

Inside the PrecisionCal app we use local secure storage (Keychain on iOS) to keep you signed in and to cache your dashboard. These are not cookies but serve a similar role and are removed when you delete the app or sign out.

5. Changes

We may update this policy and will revise the date above accordingly.

6. Contact

Questions about cookies? Email support@atkins-media.com.
